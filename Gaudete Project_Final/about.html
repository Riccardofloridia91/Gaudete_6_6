About us

We bring people together through music

Music is a magical thing that connects and bonds people together. But the most wonderful part is that not only the music can be listened to, but also created, learned, performed, shared, and discovered. Imagine that all of that you could do using only one app!

This is how our story has started. We all have had a great passion for music but could not find an app that would fulfil our needs, till the moment when we got a chance to work on this wonderful project. The future app, called Gaudete has a great potential and list of functions that will help people connect with each other from all over the world, learn how to play, teach how to play, have online concerts, find a member for a band, or just simply have fun!

If you ever wanted to find like-minded community, learn how to play an instrument, meet new people, or just listen to how other people play, do not be shy, come along on board and let’s start jamming together!
